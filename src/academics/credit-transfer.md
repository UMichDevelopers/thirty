# Credit transfer, AP, IB

See [AP and IB exam credit
information](https://admissions.umich.edu/apply/first-year-applicants/ap-ib-credit).
Note that colleges have differing requirements and policies regarding what
transfer credit can be applied to.

If you are relying on exam credit to fulfill a prerequisite to register for a
course (especially for exams taken May/June 2026), you may need to wait until
your official score report is sent and processed by U-M. This means you may
have to pick a backup class and register for your target class in July.

For dual enrollment and other courses you took at a college, refer to the
[Transfer Credit Equivalency
Search](https://transfercredit.ugadmiss.umich.edu/external). If your course
doesn't appear in the search, then it has not yet been approved and you'll be
required to submit information such as the syllabus and exams for a committee
to review to determine if it matches a course at U-M.
